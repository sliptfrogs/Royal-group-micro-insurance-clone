We offer simple, affordable and accessible insurance solutions to protect you and your loved ones, wherever life takes you.

Robang Sovattepheap

Robang Sovattepheap is a credit life insurance product that provides fixed insurance benefits against risks caused by accident or illness, which result in the insured (borrower) becoming totally and permanently disabled, partially permanently disabled, or death.

Raksmey Sovattepheap

Raksmey Sovattepheap is a life insurance product that covers risks of total and permanent disability or death caused by accident or illness. In addition, policyholders can choose an additional benefit for daily hospital allowance (Prak Somrak Pet).

Temporary Inability to Work

This insurance policy provides coverage for temporary inability to perform work due to bodily injury caused by an accident or illness. The benefits can be claimed to help repay the outstanding loan installments.
